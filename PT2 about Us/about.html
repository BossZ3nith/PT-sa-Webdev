Meet The Team

We are a group of students of UC-PNC. We aim to showcase the beauty of our school and it's suitability as an institution for learning. Every member in our group put in the time and the effort to accomplish their part in the creation of this collection. With the hope that the sights of the school grounds and nearby locations would encourage school pride, we hope that you enjoy our creation.
